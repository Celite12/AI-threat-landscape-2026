# AI Threat Landscape 2026
### A Cybersecurity Perspective

## Project Overview

This project explores the current state of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its impact on cybersecurity. It examines emerging threats, defensive strategies, governance frameworks, and real-world security implications of AI systems.

The goal is to explain complex concepts in beginner-friendly language while demonstrating cybersecurity knowledge, analytical thinking, and technical communication skills.

---

## Objectives

- Understand the current capabilities and limitations of AI systems.
- Identify AI-enabled cybersecurity threats.
- Explore defensive security controls for AI environments.
- Study governance, ethics, and risk management frameworks.
- Document findings in a way that helps both beginners and professionals.

---
